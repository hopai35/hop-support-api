# Subscription & Account Management

30Madison brands operate on a subscription model to ensure consistent treatment and convenient refills.

## Subscription Mechanics
*   **Billing Cadence**: Subscriptions are typically billed and shipped every **3 months (90 days)**.
*   **Ongoing Care Fee**: Cove charges a monthly fee for ongoing access to providers and regular check-ins (typically billed quarterly).
*   **Automatic Refills**: Orders are processed automatically to prevent interruptions in treatment.

## Managing Your Account
Patients can manage their subscriptions directly through their secure online dashboard.

### Pausing or Canceling
*   **Flexibility**: Subscriptions can be paused or canceled at any time.
*   **Process**: This can be done via the account settings/dashboard or by contacting the support team.
*   **Urgency**: If a refill is processing or about to ship (within 24 hours), patients should call support immediately to ensure the cancellation is applied before the order is finalized.

### Shipping & Delivery
*   **Shipping Fees**: Keeps often includes shipping in plan pricing. Cove typically charges a small fee (e.g., $3) unless the order is for a 90-day supply.
*   **Discreet Packaging**: All orders are shipped in discreet, unbranded packaging to protect patient privacy.
*   **Tracking**: Tracking information is emailed once the order leaves the pharmacy.

## Refund & Return Policy
*   **Final Sale**: Due to pharmacy regulations, **all prescription sales are final**.
*   **No Returns**: We cannot accept returns of prescription medications for reuse or resale.
*   **Errors**: If there is an error with an order (e.g., wrong item, damage), patients should contact support immediately for a resolution.
