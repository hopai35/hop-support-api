# Medical Consultation Flows

30Madison brands utilize a streamlined telehealth process to connect patients with licensed healthcare providers.

## The Consultation Process

### 1. Online Assessment
Patients begin by completing a comprehensive medical questionnaire. This includes:
*   Medical history and current health status.
*   Specific symptoms and concerns (e.g., hair loss patterns for Keeps, migraine frequency for Cove).
*   Current medications and potential contraindications.

### 2. Photo Submission (Keeps Specific)
For Keeps, patients are required to upload clear photos of their hair and scalp from multiple angles. This allows the provider to visualize the extent of hair loss and confirm the diagnosis.

### 3. Provider Review
A licensed physician or nurse practitioner reviews the assessment and photos.
*   **Timeline**: Reviews are typically completed within **24 to 48 hours**.
*   **Communication**: Providers may reach out via the secure messaging portal if additional information is needed.

### 4. Treatment Plan Approval
If the provider determines that treatment is appropriate, they will approve a personalized plan and issue a prescription if required.
*   **Personalization**: Plans are tailored to the individual's specific needs and medical profile.
*   **Adjustments**: Patients can message their provider at any time through their dashboard to discuss adjustments or side effects.

## Eligibility & Safety
*   **Age**: Services are generally for adults (18+).
*   **Identity Verification**: Patients must provide a valid ID to verify their identity and age.
*   **Medical Necessity**: Prescriptions are only issued if the provider deems them medically necessary and safe for the patient.
