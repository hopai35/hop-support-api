# Privacy, Security, and Trust
30Madison is committed to protecting patient privacy and ensuring the security of all Personal Health Information (PHI).

## HIPAA Compliance
As a healthcare provider, 30Madison and its brands (Keeps, Cove, etc.) adhere to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) standards.
*   **Data Protection**: We use industry-standard encryption to protect data both in transit and at rest.
*   **Secure Communications**: All medical consultations and messages with providers occur through our secure, HIPAA-compliant platform.
*   **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate need-to-know have access to patient medical records.

## PII and PHI Handling
We distinguish between Personal Identifying Information (PII) and Protected Health Information (PHI).
*   **De-identification**: For research and analytics, data is de-identified in accordance with HIPAA Safe Harbor standards.
*   **Third-Party Vendors**: Any third-party partners (e.g., pharmacies, shipping carriers) are bound by Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) to maintain the same high standards of privacy and security.

## Guardian Layer Integration (Hop Support)
For our AI-powered support, we utilize a specialized "Guardian Layer" to ensure patient trust:
*   **Automated Redaction**: Any PHI shared in chat is automatically detected and redacted before being processed by the LLM.
*   **Skyflow Vaulting**: Sensitive identifiers are vaulted in a secure Skyflow environment, allowing for reversible de-identification when a human agent needs to step in, without exposing raw data to the AI model.
*   **Audit Trails**: Every interaction is logged with full auditability to ensure compliance with healthcare regulations.

## Patient Rights
Under HIPAA, patients have specific rights regarding their information, including:
*   The right to access their medical records.
*   The right to request amendments to their records.
*   The right to receive an accounting of disclosures.
*   The right to request confidential communications.

For any privacy-related inquiries, patients can contact privacy@30madison.com.
