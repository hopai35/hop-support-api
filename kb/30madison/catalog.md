# 30Madison Product Catalogs: Keeps & Cove

30Madison operates specialized healthcare brands including Keeps (hair loss) and Cove (migraine).

## Keeps Product Catalog
Keeps provides scientifically-backed treatments for male pattern baldness.

### Prescription (Rx) Treatments
*   **Finasteride (1mg tablets)**: A daily pill that blocks the production of DHT, the hormone that causes hair loss.
*   **Ketoconazole Shampoo (2%)**: A prescription-strength shampoo used to treat dandruff and potentially assist in hair regrowth by reducing inflammation.

### Over-the-Counter (OTC) Treatments
*   **Minoxidil (5% Solution or Foam)**: A topical treatment applied twice daily to the scalp to stimulate hair follicles and promote regrowth.
*   **Thickening Shampoo & Conditioner**: Formulated to help hair look thicker and healthier.
*   **Biotin Gummies**: Dietary supplements to support healthy hair, skin, and nails.

## Cove Product Catalog
Cove offers a wide range of treatments for migraine management, including preventative and acute options.

### Preventative Medications
Daily medications intended to reduce the frequency and severity of migraine attacks.
*   Examples: Topiramate, Amitriptyline, Propranolol.

### Acute (Abortive) Medications
Medications taken at the onset of a migraine to stop the attack.
*   **Triptans**: Sumatriptan, Rizatriptan, Naratriptan, Eleatriptan.

### Anti-Nausea Medications
Used to treat the nausea and vomiting often associated with migraines.
*   Example: Ondansetron (generic Zofran).

### Dietary Supplements
Evidence-based supplements that can help manage migraines.
*   Examples: Magnesium, Riboflavin (Vitamin B2), Coenzyme Q10.
