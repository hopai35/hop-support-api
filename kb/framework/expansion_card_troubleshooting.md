# Framework - Expansion Card Troubleshooting

## Supported Expansion Cards
Framework offers USB-C, USB-A, HDMI, DisplayPort, MicroSD, and Ethernet expansion cards. All cards are hot-swappable.

## Common Issues

### Expansion card not detected
1. **Remove and reinsert** the card firmly until it clicks.
2. **Check BIOS settings**: Restart and press F2 to enter BIOS. Go to Advanced > USB Configuration and ensure USB ports are enabled.
3. **Update firmware**: Download the latest expansion card firmware from knowledgebase.frame.work.
4. **Test in another slot**: Try the card in a different expansion bay to isolate the issue.

### DisplayPort/HDMI no signal
- Ensure the card is fully seated.
- Try a different cable (some cables are incompatible with USB-C alt mode).
- Update GPU drivers: For AMD 7040 series, install Adrenalin 23.9.1+.
- The expansion card supports up to 4K@60Hz. Higher resolutions may not be supported.

### USB-C power delivery issues
- Framework USB-C expansion cards support up to 100W PD (20V/5A).
- If charging is slow, ensure you're using a 60W+ USB-C charger.
- Some third-party chargers may not be fully compatible. Use the official Framework charger for best results.

## Warranty & Replacement
Expansion cards carry a 1-year warranty. Contact support at support@frame.work for replacements.
