# Framework - DIY Assembly & Repair Guide

## Tools Required
- Phillips #0 and #1 screwdrivers (included with mainboard)
- Plastic spudger for prying
- Magnetic parts mat (recommended)

## Replacing the Keyboard
1. Power off the laptop and disconnect the battery.
2. Remove the 6 screws on the bottom panel marked with keyboard icon.
3. Flip the laptop open and gently pry the keyboard bezel starting from the top edge.
4. Disconnect the keyboard ribbon cable (lift the latch, slide cable out).
5. Install new keyboard: connect ribbon cable, press bezel into place, replace screws.

## Upgrading Memory (RAM)
- Framework supports DDR5-5600 SO-DIMM modules.
- Maximum capacity: 64GB (2x32GB).
- To upgrade: remove bottom panel, release clips on each side of RAM module, insert new module at 45-degree angle and press down.

## Upgrading Storage
- Supports M.2 NVMe (2280 or 2230 form factor).
- Remove bottom panel, unscrew the single retention screw, slide out the drive.
- For a fresh OS install, download drivers from knowledgebase.frame.work/drivers.

## Battery Replacement
- The battery is held in by 4 Phillips screws.
- Disconnect the battery connector using the pull tab.
- The Framework 55Wh battery provides approximately 8-10 hours of typical use.
