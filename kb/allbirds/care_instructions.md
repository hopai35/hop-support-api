# Allbirds - Material Care Instructions

## Wool Runners Care
- **Machine wash**: Remove insoles and laces. Wash on cold, gentle cycle.
- **Air dry only**: Do NOT put in dryer (wool can shrink).
- **Spot clean**: Use mild soap and water for small stains.
- **Frequency**: Wash every 2-4 weeks for regular use.

## Tree Dashers/Dwellers Care
- **Machine wash**: Cold water, gentle cycle.
- **Air dry**: Stuff with paper towels to maintain shape.
- **Note**: Tree material dries faster than wool.
- **Avoid bleach**: It damages the TENCEL fibers.

## Mizzle (Water-Repellent) Care
- Clean with a damp cloth.
- Do not machine wash (damages water-repellent coating).
- Re-apply water repellent spray every 3-6 months.

## Toppers (Insoles) Care
- Remove before washing your shoes.
- Hand wash with mild soap.
- Air dry completely before reinserting.

## General Tips
- Use a shoe tree to maintain shape when not wearing.
- Rotate between 2+ pairs to extend life.
- The laces are made from recycled plastic bottles - wash them in a delicates bag.
- Our wool is naturally antimicrobial (resists odor). Let shoes air out between wears.
