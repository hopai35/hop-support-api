# Allbirds - Material Science & Sustainability

## Our Materials

### SweetFoam (Sole)
- World's first carbon-negative EVA foam.
- Made from sugarcane-based green ethylene.
- Each pair sequesters carbon through renewable agriculture.
- Renewable, not petroleum-based like traditional EVA.

### Trino (Upper Material)
- A proprietary blend of TENCEL Lyocell (from eucalyptus trees) and merino wool.
- Eucalyptus pulp is sourced from FSC-certified forests.
- Uses 95% less water than conventional cotton.
- Naturally temperature-regulating and moisture-wicking.

### ZQ Merino Wool
- Ethically sourced from ZQ-certified farms in New Zealand.
- Mulesing-free and animal welfare certified.
- Biodegradable and renewable.

### Castor Bean-Based EVA
- Used in Tree Dasher midsoles.
- Castor beans require minimal water and grow on marginal land.
- Creates a lightweight, responsive cushion.

## Carbon Footprint
- Each pair of Wool Runners: approximately 4.3 kg CO2e.
- Industry average sneaker: approximately 12.6 kg CO2e.
- Allbirds offsets remaining emissions through verified carbon credits.

## Sustainability Commitments
- Goal: 50% absolute reduction in carbon footprint by 2025.
- Goal: Net zero carbon by 2030.
- All packaging is made from recycled cardboard (90% post-consumer waste).
- Free recycling program for old Allbirds shoes (returns store credit).
