# Ledger - Device Initialization & Firmware

## Setting Up Your Ledger Device

### Ledger Nano X
1. Download Ledger Live from ledger.com/live.
2. Connect your Nano X via USB-C or Bluetooth.
3. Follow the on-screen setup: choose a PIN (4-8 digits) and write down your 24-word recovery phrase.
4. **IMPORTANT**: Never enter your recovery phrase digitally. Store it offline in the provided metal card.

### Ledger Stax
1. Power on your Stax by pressing the side button.
2. Follow the E-Ink screen instructions.
3. Install the "Ledger Live" companion app on your phone.
4. Pair via Bluetooth and complete setup.

## Firmware Updates
- Always update firmware through Ledger Live (not a browser extension).
- Ensure your recovery phrase is accessible before updating.
- If the update gets stuck: close Ledger Live, unplug device, reopen Ledger Live, and retry.

## Bluetooth Pairing (Nano X)
1. In Ledger Live, go to Settings > Bluetooth and enable pairing.
2. On the Nano X, go to Settings > Bluetooth > Pair.
3. If pairing fails: reset Bluetooth on both devices and try again.

## Troubleshooting
- **Device not recognized**: Try a different USB cable. Some cables are charge-only.
- **"Connection failed" error**: Close Ledger Live, reopen, and reconnect the device.
- **Stuck on "Processing"**: This is normal during updates. Wait up to 10 minutes before restarting.
