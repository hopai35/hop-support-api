# Troubleshooting & Device Care

Keep your quip products in top shape with these simple maintenance tips.

## Electric Toothbrush
### Brush Not Vibrating
1. **Check the Battery**: Most quip brushes use a single AAA battery. Ensure it's not drained.
2. **Clean the Contacts**: Sometimes toothpaste buildup can interfere with the connection. Remove the motor and wipe the battery contacts with a dry cloth.
3. **Reset the Motor**: Remove the battery, wait 10 seconds, and re-insert it.

### Battery Replacement
1. Grip the head and the handle, and pull them apart.
2. Pull the motor out by the wings (the little plastic tabs).
3. Replace the AAA battery (positive side up).
4. Slide the motor back in and snap the head back onto the handle.

## Rechargeable Water Flosser
### Low Water Flow
- Ensure the reservoir is full and the nozzle is securely attached.
- Check for any debris in the nozzle; you can soak it in warm water with a little vinegar to remove mineral buildup.

### Charging Issues
- Ensure the magnetic charging cable is properly aligned with the charging port on the back of the device.
- Try a different USB power source.

## Smart Features
### Connection Issues
1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone.
2. Open the quip app and keep it near your brush while it's in use.
3. If the brush isn't syncing, try removing it from the app and re-pairing it.

## General Care
- **Rinse after use**: Always rinse your brush head and handle after brushing to prevent toothpaste buildup.
- **Store upright**: Use the multi-use travel cover to store your brush upright on your mirror or counter. This allows it to air dry.
- **Change heads**: Remember to swap your brush head every 3 months!
