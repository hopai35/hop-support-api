# Ingredients & Safety

At quip, we care about what goes into your mouth. Our dental care products are formulated with high-quality ingredients to provide effective care while remaining safe and pleasant to use.

## quip Anticavity Toothpaste (Mint & Watermelon)
Our toothpaste is designed to strengthen enamel and prevent cavities with a clean, refreshing taste.

### Active Ingredient
- **Sodium Fluoride (0.24%)**: Helps prevent cavities and strengthens tooth enamel.

### Inactive Ingredients
- **Sorbitol**: A sugar-free sweetener that also helps keep the toothpaste moist.
- **Water**: Used as a base.
- **Hydrated Silica**: A gentle abrasive to help remove plaque and surface stains.
- **Glycerin**: Helps maintain the texture and moisture of the toothpaste.
- **Xylitol**: A natural sweetener that helps prevent tooth decay.
- **Flavor**: Natural and artificial flavors (Mint or Watermelon).
- **Cellulose Gum**: A thickening agent.
- **Sodium Cocoyl Glutamate**: A gentle foaming agent.
- **Sodium Saccharin**: A sweetener.
- **Carrageenan**: A natural thickener derived from seaweed.
- **Titanium Dioxide**: Used for color.

## quip Refillable Mouthwash
Our mouthwash is a concentrated formula designed to be mixed with water. It's alcohol-free and gentle on your mouth.

### Active Ingredient
- **Cetylpyridinium Chloride (0.05%)**: An antibacterial agent that helps reduce plaque and gingivitis.

### Inactive Ingredients
- **Water**: Used as a base.
- **Glycerin**: Helps maintain moisture.
- **Sorbitol**: A sugar-free sweetener.
- **Xylitol**: Helps prevent tooth decay.
- **Flavor**: Fresh mint flavor.
- **Poloxamer 407**: Helps dissolve other ingredients.
- **Sodium Benzoate**: A preservative.
- **Sodium Saccharin**: A sweetener.
- **Citric Acid**: Helps balance pH levels.
- **Blue 1**: Used for color.

## quip Gum
Our sugar-free gum is designed to help maintain oral health between brushes.

### Ingredients
- **Xylitol**: A natural sweetener that helps prevent tooth decay.
- **Gum Base**: The base of the gum.
- **Natural Flavors**: For a refreshing taste.
- **Glycerin**: Keeps the gum moist.
- **Gum Arabic**: A natural thickener.
- **Spirulina Extract**: Used for natural color.
- **Carnauba Wax**: Used for a smooth finish.

## Safety Information
- **Fluoride Warning**: Keep out of reach of children under 6 years of age. If more than used for brushing is accidentally swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.
- **Alcohol-Free**: Our mouthwash is 100% alcohol-free, making it gentle and non-burning.
- **Vegan-Friendly**: All quip oral care products are vegan-friendly and never tested on animals.
