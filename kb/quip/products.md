# quip Product Guide

We believe that better oral care should be simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone. Our range of products is designed to fit your lifestyle while providing the best possible care for your teeth and gums.

## Electric Toothbrushes
- **quip Electric Toothbrush (Sonic)**: Our signature brush. Features timed sonic vibrations (2-minute timer with 30-second pulses), a sleek waterproof design, and a multi-use travel cover that doubles as a mirror mount.
- **Smart Electric Toothbrush**: All the features of our standard brush, plus Bluetooth connectivity to the quip app. Track your brushing habits, get personalized tips, and earn rewards for good technique.
- **Kids Electric Toothbrush**: Designed specifically for smaller mouths. Features a smaller brush head, a grippier handle, and a gentle motor.

## Refills & Brush Heads
We offer several types of replacement heads to suit your needs:
- **Standard Sonic Brush Head**: Soft bristles that are gentle on gums.
- **Extra Soft Brush Head**: For those with sensitive teeth or gums.
- **Whitening Brush Head**: Features unique polishing bristles.
- **Kid Sonic Brush Head**: Sized for smaller mouths.

## Flossing Solutions
- **Rechargeable Water Flosser**: Cordless and waterproof with two pressure modes.
- **Refillable Floss String**: A sleek, reusable dispenser with expandible, vegan mint-flavored floss.
- **Refillable Floss Pick**: A reusable pick with easy-to-replace floss heads.

## Care & Accessories
- **Anticavity Toothpaste**: Available in Mint and Watermelon flavors. Contains fluoride to help prevent cavities and strengthen enamel.
- **Refillable Mouthwash**: A concentrated formula that you mix with water in our sleek, reusable glass carafe.
- **Gum**: Sugar-free gum with Xylitol to help maintain oral health between brushes.

## Tone & Voice
When speaking about quip products, always be **friendly, encouraging, and clear**. We want to make oral care feel like a reward, not a chore!
