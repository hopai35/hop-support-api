# Quip Warranty & Logistics Policy

## Limited Warranty
Quip products are covered by a 1-year limited warranty against hardware defects.
- **Coverage:** Motor failure, vibration issues, charging defects.
- **Exclusions:** Normal wear and tear, accidental damage (e.g., dropping the brush).

## Automated Warranty Claim Flow
If your Quip brush stops working:
1. AI will guide you through troubleshooting (Battery replacement, cleaning contacts).
2. If troubleshooting fails, AI verifies your original order date via our ERP.
3. If within warranty, AI automatically processes a -bash replacement order and confirms your shipping address.

## Subscription Logistics
Refill heads and floss are delivered every 3 months.
- **Tracking:** AI retrieves tracking numbers for your latest refill.
- **Inventory Sync:** If you have too many refills, you can skip your next delivery via the AI chat.
