# Quip Subscription Management

Welcome to the quip family! We're here to help you maintain a healthy oral care routine effortlessly.

## How it Works
Our subscription model ensures you never run out of the essentials. By default, we ship replacement brush heads and other refills every **3 months**.

## Benefits
- **Automatic Deliveries**: Fresh supplies arrive at your door just when you need them.
- **Lifetime Warranty**: As long as you have an active subscription, your quip electric toothbrush is covered by our lifetime warranty.
- **Discounts**: Get 15% off your first order when you sign up for a subscription.

## Managing Your Subscription
You can manage your subscription at any time by logging into your account at [getquip.com](https://www.getquip.com).
- **Update Frequency**: While 3 months is the standard, you can adjust your delivery schedule in your account settings.
- **Pause or Cancel**: You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time with no hidden fees.
- **Add Items**: Want to try our mouthwash or gum? You can easily add new products to your existing subscription.

## Friendly Reminder
Consistency is key to a great smile! Our 3-month schedule aligns with dentist recommendations for changing your brush head.
