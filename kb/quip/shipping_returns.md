# Shipping & Returns

We want to make sure your quip experience is smooth from start to finish. Here's everything you need to know about getting your products and what to do if they're not quite right.

## Shipping
- **Subscription Orders**: Shipping is always free for subscription refill orders!
- **Store Orders**: For one-time purchases, shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected speed.
- **Delivery Time**: Most orders arrive within 3-5 business days after processing. You'll receive a tracking number via email as soon as your package ships.

## Returns
- **30-Day Guarantee**: If you're not happy with your quip, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
- **How to Start a Return**: Contact our support team or visit our online returns portal to generate a prepaid return label.
- **Refunds**: Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

## Warranty
- **Lifetime Warranty**: As long as you have an active subscription for your quip electric toothbrush, it's covered by a lifetime warranty. If it stops working, we'll replace it for free!
- **Limited Warranty**: For one-time purchases without a subscription, we offer a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase.

## Damaged or Missing Items
If your order arrives damaged or is missing items, please let us know within 48 hours of delivery, and we'll make it right.
