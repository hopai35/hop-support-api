# Magic Spoon Subscription & Support Guide

## 100% Happiness Guarantee
We want you to love your cereal. If you don't, we offer a full refund on your first case (4 boxes) within 30 days of delivery, even if the boxes are opened.

## Managing Subscriptions
- **Flavor Mix:** You can change your flavor selection for every shipment. Log in, go to "Manage Subscription," and select "Edit Flavors."
- **Frequency:** Choose between 30, 45, or 60-day intervals.
- **Skip/Pause:** Accessible via the portal anytime before the next billing date.

## Shipping & Logistics
- **Carriers:** Primarily UPS and FedEx.
- **Free Shipping:** Always included for subscribers.
- **Tracking:** Sent via email upon shipment.

## Support Integration (Gorgias)
Our AI agent is fully integrated with Gorgias. It can look up your order history, change your upcoming flavor mix, and process address changes in real-time.
