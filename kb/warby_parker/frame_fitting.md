# Warby Parker - Frame Fitting & Adjustment

## Finding Your Size
Warby Parker frames come in three width sizes:
- **Narrow**: Best for PD under 58mm
- **Medium**: Best for PD 58-64mm
- **Wide**: Best for PD over 64mm

Each frame listing includes:
- Lens width (e.g., 49mm)
- Bridge width (e.g., 20mm)
- Temple length (e.g., 145mm)

## DIY Adjustments
### Tightening Loose Glasses
1. Use the mini screwdriver included with your glasses.
2. Tighten the hinge screw on each temple.
3. Be careful not to overtighten.

### How to Fix Glasses That Slip Down
- Visit any retail location for a free adjustment.
- Or gently bend the temple ends (behind the ears) slightly inward.
- Temple tips can be warmed under warm water for 30 seconds to make them more pliable.
