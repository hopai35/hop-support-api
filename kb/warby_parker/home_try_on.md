# Warby Parker - Home Try-On Program

## How Home Try-On Works
1. Select 5 frames online (free shipping both ways).
2. Receive your box within 5-7 business days.
3. Try them on at home for 5 days.
4. Use the prepaid return label to send them back.

## Trial Period
- You have 5 days from delivery to try on your frames.
- If you need more time, contact us for a free extension.
- Late returns may result in a charge for the frames.

## What If I Damage a Trial Frame?
- Don't worry - small accidents happen.
- You won't be charged for minor damage to trial frames.
- If the frame is severely damaged or lost, we may charge a replacement fee.

## After Your Try-On
- Order your favorites online.
- Choose from single-vision, progressive, or blue-light lenses.
- Use your FSA/HSA card for eligible purchases.

## Returns on Purchased Glasses
- 30-day return policy on all glasses.
- Free return shipping.
- Full refund (minus prescription lens cost for progressive or custom lenses).
- Eyeglass cleaning cloths and cases are yours to keep.
