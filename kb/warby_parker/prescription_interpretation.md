# Warby Parker - Prescription Interpretation

## Understanding Your Prescription

### Key Terms
- **OD** (Oculus Dexter): Right eye
- **OS** (Oculus Sinister): Left eye
- **SPH** (Sphere): The main lens power (- for nearsighted, + for farsighted). Measured in diopters.
- **CYL** (Cylinder): Corrects astigmatism. Present if you have astigmatism.
- **Axis**: The orientation of astigmatism correction (0-180 degrees).
- **PD** (Pupillary Distance): Distance between your pupils in mm. Critical for proper lens centering.

### Reading Your Rx
Example: OD: -2.50 SPH, -0.75 CYL, 180 Axis
- Right eye: 2.5 diopters nearsighted, with 0.75 diopters of astigmatism at 180 degrees

### How to Upload Your Prescription
1. Go to warbyparker.com/upload.
2. Take a clear photo of your prescription or enter it manually.
3. Our team verifies your prescription within 1 business day.

### What If I Don't Have My PD?
- PD is often NOT on your prescription.
- We'll send you a measuring kit or you can use our online PD tool.
- Average PD is 54-68mm.
- An incorrect PD causes discomfort but won't damage your eyes.

## Common Questions

### Can I use an expired prescription?
- Prescriptions expire after 1-2 years (varies by state).
- We require valid prescriptions for all orders.

### Do I need a prescription for non-prescription glasses?
- No! You can buy blue light glasses or non-prescription frames without one.
