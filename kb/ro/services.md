# Ro Services Overview

Ro is a direct-to-consumer telehealth company that handles everything from diagnosis to the convenient delivery of medication. We provide specialized care across several health categories.

## Health Categories & Treatments

### Weight Loss
*   **The Ro Body Membership**: A comprehensive program including provider care and GLP-1 medications.
*   **Medications**: Wegovy® (pill & pen), Zepbound®, Ozempic®, Saxenda®, and Foundayo™ pill.

### Sexual Health
*   **Erectile Dysfunction (ED)**: Treatments include Ro Sparks, Daily Rise Gummies, Viagra®, Sildenafil (generic Viagra), Cialis®, and Tadalafil (generic Cialis).
*   **Premature Ejaculation (PE)**: Roman Swipes and specialized consultations.
*   **Performance Support**: Testosterone Support Supplements.

### Hair Loss
*   **For Men**: Ro Mane Spray, Oral Finasteride, Oral Minoxidil, Topical Minoxidil, Revive Shampoo, and Restore Conditioner.
*   **For Women**: Oral Minoxidil and Hair Solution Rx.
*   **Lashes**: LATISSE® for longer lashes.

### Fertility
*   **Testing**: Fertility Hormone Test, Ovulation Test, and Pregnancy Test.
*   **Supplements**: Prenatal Multivitamins.
*   **Other**: Sperm Kit (member login required).

### Skin & Dermatology
*   **Custom Skincare**: Personalized Rx skincare treatments for acne, photoaging, and more.
*   **Stop Outbreaks**: Treatments for Cold Sores and Genital Herpes (Valacyclovir).

### Daily Health
*   **Supplements**: Men's Daily Multivitamin, Prenatal Multivitamin, and Testosterone Support.

## How it Works
1.  **Online Visit**: Complete a health assessment from the comfort of your home. In most cases, the online visit is free.
2.  **Diagnosis**: A licensed doctor or nurse practitioner reviews your information and provides a diagnosis.
3.  **Treatment Plan**: If appropriate, a personalized treatment plan is approved.
4.  **Delivery**: Medications and products are delivered conveniently to your door.
