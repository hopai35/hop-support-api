# Ro Weight Loss Program

Ro provides a comprehensive weight loss program, primarily centered around the **Ro Body Membership** and high-efficacy GLP-1 medications.

## The Ro Body Membership
The Ro Body membership is designed for long-term weight management. It includes:
*   **Provider Care**: Ongoing access to licensed healthcare providers.
*   **GLP-1 Medications**: Access to prescribed medications like Wegovy® or Ozempic® (based on eligibility).
*   **Insurance Concierge**: Assistance with insurance coverage for GLP-1 medications.
*   **Ongoing Support**: Tools and resources to track your progress.

## Available Medications
We offer several GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) receptor agonists and other weight loss treatments:
*   **Wegovy®**: Available as both a pen (injection) and a NEW pill version.
*   **Zepbound®**: Available as a pen and the NEW Zepbound® KwikPen®.
*   **Ozempic®**: FDA-approved for Type 2 diabetes but often prescribed off-label for weight loss.
*   **Saxenda®**: An injectable GLP-1 for weight management.
*   **Foundayo™**: A NEW pill version for weight management.

## Insurance & Pricing
*   **Ro Body Membership**: Involves a monthly membership fee.
*   **Medication Cost**: The cost of the medication is separate from the membership fee.
*   **Insurance**: Our insurance concierge service works to help you get coverage for your medication, which can significantly reduce out-of-pocket costs.
*   **GLP-1 Supply Tracker**: We provide tools to help you track the availability of GLP-1 medications given recent supply chain shortages.
