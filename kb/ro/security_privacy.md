# Security & Privacy

At Ro, we take the privacy and security of your health information very seriously.

## Data Protection
*   **Privacy Law Compliance**: The Ro platform is designed to comply with all relevant privacy laws (including HIPAA-related standards for handling protected health information).
*   **Security Protocols**: We have implemented strict security protocols and encryption to protect your personal and medical information.

## Account Safety
*   **Secure Sign-In**: We provide tools to help you securely access your account. If you have trouble signing in, you can use our [Account Recovery](https://ro.co/contact-us/trouble-signing-in/) tools.
*   **Information Usage**: Ro processes your personal data to provide our services, show relevant ads, and send offers. You can customize your privacy preferences at any time.

## Your Privacy Choices
*   **Transparency**: We are transparent about how we use cookies and tracking technologies.
*   **Customization**: Members can manage their privacy preferences and "Do Not Sell" choices through their account settings or the "Your Privacy Choices" link on our website.

For more detailed information, please review our full **Privacy Policy** at [ro.co/privacy-policy/](https://ro.co/privacy-policy/).
