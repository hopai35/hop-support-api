# Shipping & Returns Policy

## Shipping Information
*   **Delivery Convenience**: Products and medications are delivered directly to your door.
*   **Signatures**: For the convenience of our members, signatures are **not required** upon delivery for most orders.
*   **Shipping Cadence**: 
    *   Orders are shipped on weekdays only.
    *   Orders placed after 5:00 PM will ship the next business day.
*   **Tracking**: Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking link to monitor your shipment.

## Returns & Refunds
*   **Prescription Products**: Due to health and safety regulations, we **cannot accept returns** of prescription products for reuse or resale.
*   **Final Sale**: All sales are final.
*   **Errors**: If you believe there has been an error in the filling of your prescription, please contact us immediately at **care@ro.co** or through the Contact Us page on our website.

## Managing Shipments
Members have full control over their shipping schedule through their account dashboard:
*   **Turn off auto-refill**: Stop future automatic shipments.
*   **Delay shipment**: Reschedule your next delivery date.
*   **Cancel recent requests**: If you have just submitted a treatment request and wish to cancel, contact the Care Team as soon as possible before the order enters processing.
