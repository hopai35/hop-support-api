# Subscriptions, Billing, & Insurance

## Billing Policy
*   **Initial Online Visit**: In most cases, your initial online visit is free.
*   **When You Are Charged**: You are only charged for medication after a doctor or nurse practitioner has approved your personalized treatment plan.
*   **All Sales Final**: All sales are final. We cannot accept returns of prescription products for reuse or resale.

## Insurance Coverage
*   **General Policy**: Most services, products, and medications offered through Ro are **not covered by insurance**.
*   **Weight Loss Exception**: For the Ro Body membership, we provide an Insurance Concierge service to help you seek coverage for the specific GLP-1 medication from your insurance provider.

## Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
*   **Credit/Debit**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
*   **Digital Wallets**: PayPal, Google Pay (on Chrome), and Apple Pay (on Safari).
*   **FSA/HSA**: We do **not** accept HSA/FSA cards at this time. However, you can submit a detailed receipt of your purchase to your healthcare provider for potential reimbursement.

## Managing Your Subscription
You can manage your shipments and account by visiting the "Contact Us" page or your member dashboard.

### Pausing or Canceling
*   **Auto-Refill**: You can stop future orders from shipping automatically by turning off your auto-refill in your account settings.
*   **Delaying Shipments**: You can change your automatic shipment date if you need more time between orders.
*   **Canceling Processing Orders**: If an order is currently processing, contact the Care Team immediately. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be canceled or returned.

## Contacting Support
For any questions regarding billing or your account, please reach out to **care@ro.co**.
