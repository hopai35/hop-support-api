# Ritual - Ingredient Traceability

## Traceability Philosophy
Every ingredient in Ritual products is traceable from source to capsule. We provide the scientific rationale, sourcing location, and clinical evidence for each nutrient.

## Key Ingredients & Sourcing

### Vitamin D3 (1,000 IU)
- **Source**: Lanolin from sheep's wool (New Zealand)
- **Form**: Vegan-friendly alternative available (lichen-derived)
- **Role**: Bone health, immune function, mood support
- **Bioavailability**: Enhanced with MCT oil for fat-soluble absorption

### Omega-3 DHA (600mg)
- **Source**: Microalgae (Schizochytrium sp.) grown in controlled fermenters
- **Why algae**: Sustainable, no fishy aftertaste, vegan
- **Role**: Brain health, prenatal development
- **Clinical Evidence**: Algal DHA shows equivalent bioavailability to fish oil

### Iron (18mg)
- **Source**: Ferrous bisglycinate (chelated form)
- **Why this form**: 4x better absorption than ferrous sulfate, no GI discomfort
- **Role**: Red blood cell formation, energy metabolism
- **Note**: Delayed-release capsule prevents stomach upset

### Methylated B Vitamins
- **Vitamin B12 (50mcg)**: Methylcobalamin (active form)
- **Folate (400mcg DFE)**: L-Methylfolate (active form)
- **Why methylated**: Immediately usable by the body, no conversion needed
- **Ideal for**: MTHFR gene variant carriers (30-40% of population)
