# Ritual Subscription FAQ

## How do I manage my subscription?
You can manage your subscription directly through your Ritual account or by asking our AI assistant. Common actions include:
- **Tracking a shipment:** Ask "Where is my order?" to get real-time tracking from Shopify.
- **Delaying a shipment:** If you have extra vitamins, you can delay your next delivery by 7, 14, or 30 days.
- **Changing products:** Switch between Essential for Women, Essential for Men, or our Postnatal lines at any time.

## Kustomer Integration
Hop Support is fully integrated with our Kustomer CRM. When you message us, the AI automatically retrieves:
- Your active subscriptions.
- Your last 3 orders.
- Any previous support interactions.

## ROI of AI Support
By automating over 60% of routine subscription queries, Ritual's CX team (the "Clinical Concierge") can focus on complex nutritional inquiries that require human expertise.
