# Hims & Hers - Treatment Guide: Topical Products

## How to Apply Topical Treatments
1. Clean and dry the affected area before application.
2. Apply a thin layer as directed by your provider.
3. Wash hands thoroughly after application.
4. Allow the product to dry before dressing.

## Finasteride & Minoxidil Topical (Hims Hair)
- **Application**: Apply 1mL to the scalp once daily.
- **Timing**: Use at the same time each day for best results.
- **What to expect**: Initial shedding may occur in the first 2-6 weeks. This is normal and indicates the treatment is working. Visible results typically appear after 3-6 months of consistent use.
- **Missed dose**: If you miss a dose, apply it as soon as you remember. If it's almost time for your next dose, skip the missed one. Do not double up.

## Tretinoin (Skin Care)
- **Application**: Pea-sized amount to clean, dry face once daily (typically at night).
- **Sun sensitivity**: Use SPF 30+ sunscreen daily. Tretinoin increases sun sensitivity.
- **Purge period**: Some users experience initial purging (breakouts) for 4-6 weeks. This resolves with continued use.
- **Moisturizer**: Apply moisturizer 20 minutes after tretinoin to reduce irritation.

## Storage Instructions
- Store all topical treatments at room temperature (68-77°F / 20-25°C).
- Keep away from direct sunlight, heat, and moisture.
- Do not freeze.
- Keep out of reach of children and pets.
