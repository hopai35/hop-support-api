# Hims & Hers - Subscription & Billing Guide

## Subscription Plans
Hims & Hers offers monthly and multi-month subscription plans:
- **Monthly**: Auto-renewed each month. Best for trying a new treatment.
- **3-Month**: Save 15% per month. Auto-renewed every 3 months.
- **6-Month**: Save 25% per month. Auto-renewed every 6 months. Best value.

## How Subscriptions Work
1. After your initial consultation and prescription approval, your subscription activates.
2. Your first shipment arrives within 5-7 business days.
3. Subsequent refills ship automatically based on your plan.
4. You'll receive email/SMS reminders 7 days before each shipment.

## Billing Information
- **Payment Methods**: Credit/debit card, HSA/FSA cards accepted.
- **Billing Cycle**: Charged at the start of each subscription period.
- **Receipts**: Available in your account dashboard under "Billing History".
- **Tax**: Sales tax applied based on your shipping address.

## Changing Your Plan
- **Upgrade**: You can upgrade to a multi-month plan anytime for better value.
- **Downgrade**: Downgrade requests take effect at the next billing cycle.
- **Pause**: You can pause your subscription for 1-3 months. Log in to your account to manage.

## Cancellation Policy
- Cancel anytime from your account dashboard.
- Current period remains active — you'll receive any already-paid-for shipments.
- No cancellation fees.
- To restart, simply log in and reactivate.

## Insurance and HSA/FSA
- **HSA/FSA**: Eligible expenses. Use your card at checkout or update in billing settings.
- **Insurance**: Hims & Hers does not directly bill insurance. You pay out-of-pocket. A superbill may be available for eligible visits.
- **Superbill**: Request a superbill from your account for potential insurance reimbursement.

## Failed Payments
- If a payment fails, you'll receive an email notification.
- Your subscription has a 5-day grace period to update payment info.
- After 3 failed attempts, the subscription is paused until payment is resolved.
- Update payment methods in your account dashboard under "Billing".
