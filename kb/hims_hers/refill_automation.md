# Hims & Hers - Refill Automation

## How Refills Work
1. Hims & Hers subscriptions auto-refill based on your treatment plan.
2. You'll receive a notification 7 days before each refill ships.
3. Refills can be managed in your account dashboard.

## Refill Statuses
- **Active**: Your subscription is on track. Next refill scheduled.
- **Processing**: Your refill is being prepared by the pharmacy.
- **Shipped**: Your refill is on the way (tracking provided via email).
- **Delayed**: Usually due to payment or address verification. We'll notify you.
- **Paused**: You've temporarily stopped refills.

## Managing Refills via AI
The AI can help with:
- Checking next refill date
- Updating shipping address
- Changing payment method
- Pausing refills (1-3 months)
- Checking order status

## What the AI CANNOT Do
- Change prescription dosage or medication
- Expedite pharmacy processing
- Override clinical holds
- Process insurance changes (requires human agent)

## Refill Automation Rules
- Hard Pause: Clinical hold requires human verification
- Soft Pause: Customer-requested pause (<3 months, auto-processed)
- Address Change: New address verified before shipment
- Payment Failure: 3 retries before subscription hold
