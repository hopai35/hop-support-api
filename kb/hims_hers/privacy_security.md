# Privacy and Security at Hims & Hers
We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your personal and health information.

## HIPAA and Data Privacy
Hims & Hers is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of Protected Health Information (PHI) in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).
*   **Encryption**: We use advanced encryption technologies (SSL/TLS) to protect your data during transmission and at rest.
*   **Secure Infrastructure**: Our platform is built on secure, HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with strict access controls.
*   **Confidentiality**: Your medical records and consultations are strictly confidential and only accessible to authorized healthcare providers and support staff.

## Protection of Personal Information
*   **Data Minimization**: We only collect the information necessary to provide our telehealth services and fulfill your prescriptions.
*   **Third-Party Oversight**: All Business Associates (e.g., partner pharmacies, labs) are vetted for compliance and are legally bound to protect your data.
*   **No Selling of PHI**: We do not sell your protected health information to third parties.

## Secure Telehealth Platform
All interactions with licensed medical providers occur within our secure messaging portal or via encrypted video calls. This ensures that your clinical discussions remain private and secure.

## Your Rights as a Patient
Under HIPAA, you have rights regarding your health information, including:
*   Reviewing and obtaining a copy of your health records.
*   Requesting corrections to your information.
*   Knowing how your information is shared.

For detailed information, please see our full Privacy Policy on our website.
