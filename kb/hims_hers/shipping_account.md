# Hims & Hers - Shipping & Account Management

## Shipping Information
- **Standard Shipping**: Free. Delivers in 5-7 business days.
- **Express Shipping**: $9.99. Delivers in 2-3 business days.
- **Shipping Carrier**: Shipments are sent via discreet, unbranded packaging.
- **Shipping Days**: Monday through Friday (excluding holidays).
- **PO Boxes**: Not accepted. Physical street address required.

## Discreet Packaging
All Hims & Hers shipments arrive in discreet, plain packaging with no branding or product information on the outside. The return address reads "Fulfillment Center" with no company name.

## Prescription Verification
- All prescriptions require an active consultation with a licensed provider.
- Prescriptions are valid for up to 12 months, with periodic check-ins required.
- You'll receive a notification when your check-in is due.

## Account Management

### Creating an Account
1. Visit hims.com or hers.com.
2. Complete a brief health questionnaire.
3. A licensed provider reviews your information (typically within 24 hours).
4. Once approved, select your treatment plan and subscribe.

### Updating Account Information
- **Shipping Address**: Update in Account Settings. Changes must be made at least 48 hours before next shipment.
- **Payment Method**: Update in Billing Settings. Saved cards can be managed there.
- **Email/Password**: Update in Account Settings under Security.

### Provider Communication
- Message your provider directly through your account dashboard.
- Typical response time: 1-2 business days.
- Urgent medical concerns should not be sent via messaging — call 911 for emergencies.

### Check-Ins and Follow-Ups
- Periodic check-ins are required to maintain your prescription.
- You'll receive email reminders when a check-in is due.
- Complete the brief questionnaire to continue without interruption.
- If you miss a check-in, your prescription may be paused.

## Data Privacy
- All health information is encrypted and stored securely (HIPAA-compliant).
- Your data is never shared without your explicit consent.
- You can request deletion of your data at any time through your account.
- For full details, see our Privacy Policy at hims.com/privacy.
