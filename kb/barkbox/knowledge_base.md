# BarkBox Subscription & Logistics Excellence

## Subscription Plans & Renewal
BarkBox offers Monthly, 6-month, and 12-month plans.
- **Renewal:** All plans auto-renew. To prevent renewal, disable it in "Subscription Settings" before the 1st of the renewal month.
- **Commitment:** Multi-month plans (6/12) involve a commitment. Cancellation stops renewal but doesn't refund remaining boxes in the term.

## Customization & Upgrades
- **Super Chewer Upgrade:** If your dog destroys toys too fast, you can upgrade to "Super Chewer" (rubber/nylon toys) via account settings or support.
- **Allergy Tailoring:** We can accommodate allergies to Chicken, Beef, and Turkey. Update this in "Dog Profile".
- **Toy/Treat Only:** Options are available for "Toys Only" or "Double Toys" boxes.

## Shipping & Delivery
- **Carriers:** We use a combination of FedEx, USPS, and DHL (for last-mile delivery).
- **Tracking:** Numbers are generated around the 15th of the month.
- **Canada/AK/HI:** Shipping is $8 per box.

## Scout's Honor Guarantee
If your dog doesn't like an item, we replace it for free. No return necessary. This applies to both toys and treats.
