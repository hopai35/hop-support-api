# BarkBox - Subscription Guide

## How BarkBox Works
1. **Tell us about your dog**: Take the dog profile quiz (breed, size, age, chew style, allergies).
2. **We curate a box**: Each month, we pack 2 premium toys, 2 bags of treats, and 1 chew.
3. **It arrives at your door**: Box ships around the 15th of each month.
4. **Your dog goes nuts**: Every box is themed with a fun design and personality.

## Subscription Plans
- **Monthly**: $35/month. Cancel or skip anytime.
- **6-Month**: $23/month ($138 total). Best value.
- **12-Month**: $20/month ($240 total). Ultimate commitment.

## Customizing Your Box
- **Chew Style**: Light, moderate, or heavy — we match toys to your dog's destruction level.
- **Size**: Extra small (under 10 lbs), small (10-25 lbs), medium (25-50 lbs), large (50+ lbs).
- **Treat Preference**: Soft chews, crunchy biscuits, or a mix.
- **Allergies**: Note any food allergies in your dog's profile.
- **Toy Preference**: Plush, rope, rubber, or a mix.

## Managing Your Subscription
- **Skip a month**: Log in and select "Skip" before the 10th of the month.
- **Pause**: Pause for 1-3 months. Auto-resumes after.
- **Cancel**: Cancel anytime. Your current box still ships if already processed.
- **Restart**: Reactivate from your account page.

## Shipping
- **Delivery**: Ships within 3-5 business days of the billing date.
- **Tracking**: Tracking info sent via email when your box ships.
- **Address Changes**: Update before the 5th of the month for the next box.
- **International**: US and Canada only at this time.
