# BarkBox - Treat Information

## Treat Types
BarkBox includes a variety of treats in every box:
- **Soft Chews**: Grain-free, moist texture. Great for training and seniors.
- **Crunchy Biscuits**: Oven-baked, satisfying crunch. Good for dental health.
- **Freeze-Dried**: Single-ingredient meat treats. High-value reward.
- **Sticks & Chews**: Long-lasting dental chews.

## Ingredients
- All treats are made with natural ingredients.
- No corn, wheat, or soy in standard recipes.
- No artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives.
- Primary proteins: chicken, beef, peanut butter, sweet potato, pumpkin.

## Common Allergens
- **Chicken**: Most common allergen. We can exclude chicken from your box.
- **Peanut Butter**: Some dogs are allergic. Let us know for alternatives.
- **Grain**: Grain-free options available by default.
- **Beef**: Can be excluded on request.

## Nutritional Information
- Treats are designed to be complementary to a complete diet.
- Feed in moderation — treats should not exceed 10% of daily calories.
- Calorie counts vary by treat type (typically 5-25 kcal per treat).
- Specific nutritional breakdown available on each treat's packaging.

## BarkBox Treat Standards
- **Made in**: USA and Canada.
- **Sourcing**: Ingredients from trusted suppliers.
- **Testing**: Every batch tested for quality and safety.
- **No recalls**: BarkBox has maintained a zero-recall record.

## Treat Substitutions
- If your dog can't eat certain ingredients, update your profile.
- We can substitute treats in your box based on dietary needs.
- For severe allergies, please contact support for a personalized plan.
