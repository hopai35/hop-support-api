# BarkBox - Toy Safety & Chew Guide

## Toy Materials
BarkBox toys are made from:
- **Plush**: Double-stitched, no-stuffing designs to reduce mess.
- **Rope**: Natural cotton fibers. Great for dental health.
- **Rubber**: Food-grade silicone. Best for heavy chewers.
- **Nylon**: Durable chew toys for power chewers.

## Matching Toys to Your Dog's Chew Style

### Light Chewer (Puppies, seniors, small breeds)
- **Best toys**: Plush, soft squeakers, lightweight rope.
- **Avoid**: Hard nylon or extreme rubber (too tough for gentle mouths).
- **Expected toy life**: Weeks to months.

### Moderate Chewer (Most adult dogs)
- **Best toys**: Plush with reinforced seams, medium rope knots, rubber treat dispensers.
- **Mix it up**: Variety extends toy life and keeps your dog engaged.
- **Expected toy life**: 1-4 weeks per toy.

### Heavy Chewer (Pit bulls, shepherds, huskies)
- **Best toys**: Extreme rubber, knotted rope, nylon bones.
- **Avoid**: Plush toys (destroyed in minutes). Opt for the "Super Chewer" box upgrade.
- **Expected toy life**: Hours to days for standard toys.
- **Super Chewer**: Heavy chewers should upgrade to BarkBox Super Chewer for heavy-duty toys.

## Safety Guidelines
- Always supervise your dog with new toys.
- Remove and replace damaged toys (loose parts, torn seams).
- Choose size-appropriate toys (too small = choking hazard).
- No toy is indestructible. Inspect regularly.

## Treat Safety
- All BarkBox treats are made in the USA/Canada.
- Grain-free and limited ingredient options available.
- Always introduce new treats gradually.
- Check ingredient labels for allergens.
- If your dog has known allergies, note them in your profile.

## When to Contact Support
- Toy arrives damaged: We'll send a replacement immediately.
- Your dog's chew style changed: Update your profile.
- Allergic reaction to treat: Contact vet immediately, then notify us.
