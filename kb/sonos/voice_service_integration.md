# Sonos - Voice Service Integration

## Supported Voice Assistants
Sonos works with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Sonos Voice Control.

### Setting Up Alexa on Sonos
1. Open the Sonos app and go to Settings > Voice Services.
2. Select "Amazon Alexa" and tap "Add to Sonos".
3. You'll be redirected to the Alexa app for account linking.
4. Choose which room(s) you want Alexa in.

### Setting Up Google Assistant
1. In the Sonos app, go to Settings > Voice Services > Google Assistant.
2. Link your Google Account.
3. Select your home and rooms.
4. Note: Google Assistant works on specific Sonos devices (Arc, Beam, Era 300/100, Move 2, One, One SL).

### Sonos Voice Control
- Built-in, privacy-focused voice control.
- Activated by saying "Hey Sonos".
- Can control music playback, volume, and groups.
- No personal data collected - completely private.

## Troubleshooting Voice Services

### Alexa/Google not responding
1. Ensure the microphone is enabled (green light on).
2. Check that the voice service is set up in the Sonos app.
3. Reboot the Sonos device.
4. Ensure your Sonos is connected to the internet.

### "Command failed" errors
- Rephrase the command (be more specific about the room or device).
- Check that your music service is still linked and active.
- Voice control may not work with all music services. Confirm compatibility.

### Privacy
- Sonos Voice Control processes all commands locally on-device.
- Alexa and Google send commands to cloud services.
- Press the microphone button to physically disconnect (on supported devices).
