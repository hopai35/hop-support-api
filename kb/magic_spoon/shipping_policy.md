# Magic Spoon Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Replacement Policy (Crushed/Damaged Cereal)
At Magic Spoon, we want your breakfast to arrive in perfect condition. If your cereal was crushed, the box was damaged, or the seal was broken during shipping:
1. Contact our support agent.
2. Provide a photo of the damaged item.
3. Our AI assistant can automatically issue a replacement order (no-charge) via Gorgias/Shopify if the report is within 7 days of delivery.

## Gorgias Integration
Hop Support is embedded directly in our Gorgias help desk. AI can:
- Track delivery status via carrier APIs.
- Issue replacements for damaged shipments.
- Update shipping addresses if the order hasn't left the warehouse.

## WISMO (Where Is My Order?)
If your tracking says "Delivered" but the cereal hasn't arrived, the AI will check the carrier's proof-of-delivery photo and, if needed, start a human agent escalation for a carrier claim.
