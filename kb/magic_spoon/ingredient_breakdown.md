# Magic Spoon - Ingredient Breakdown

## What Makes Magic Spoon Different

### High Protein (12-13g per serving)
- Source: Milk protein isolate (from whey and casein).
- Why: Keeps you full longer, supports muscle maintenance.
- Compared to regular cereal: 4-5x more protein.

### Zero Sugar
- Sweetened with: Allulose, monk fruit, and stevia.
- Allulose: A rare sugar found naturally in figs and raisins. 90% fewer calories than sugar.
- Monk fruit: Natural sweetener, zero calories, zero glycemic impact.
- Stevia: Plant-based, zero calories.
- Net effect: Sweet taste without blood sugar spike.

### Low Carb / Keto-Friendly
- Net carbs: 3-4g per serving.
- Fiber: 2g per serving (from chicory root fiber).
- Suitable for keto, paleo, low-carb, and diabetic-friendly diets.

### Grain-Free
- Base: Made from a proprietary blend of milk protein and tapioca fiber.
- No corn, wheat, rice, oats, or other grains.
- Gluten-free certified.
- Paleo-friendly.

## Full Ingredient List (Classic Fruity)
Milk protein isolate, tapioca fiber, allulose, sunflower oil, natural flavors, monk fruit extract, stevia extract, salt, vitamin E (mixed tocopherols for freshness).

## Allergen Information
- **Contains**: Milk (whey, casein).
- **Does NOT contain**: Gluten, soy, eggs, wheat, corn, nuts (processed in a facility that handles tree nuts).
- **Cross-contamination risk**: Facility processes tree nuts. Trace amounts possible.
- **Lactose**: Minimal. Most lactose-sensitive individuals tolerate Magic Spoon well.

## Storage
- Store in a cool, dry place.
- Reseal bag after opening.
- Best consumed within 2 weeks of opening for peak crunch.
