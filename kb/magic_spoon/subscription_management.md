# Magic Spoon - Subscription Management

## Subscription Plans
- **Flexible Monthly**: Delivered every 4 weeks. Cancel or skip anytime.
- **6-Pack Subscription**: 6 boxes delivered one per month. Save 10%.
- **Variety Subscription**: Mix and match flavors each month.

## How Subscriptions Work
1. Choose your flavor(s) and plan.
2. Your first box ships within 3-5 business days.
3. Subsequent boxes auto-deliver every 4 weeks.
4. You'll receive a shipping notification with tracking.

## Managing Your Subscription

### Change Flavors
- Log in to your account and select "Change Flavors."
- Changes apply to your next shipment.

### Skip a Delivery
- Login before the billing date and select "Skip."
- Your delivery is skipped once. Subscription resumes automatically.
- Must skip before the billing date (usually 5 days before shipping).

### Pause
- Pause for 1-3 months from your account settings.
- Your subscription pauses after the current paid period.
- Auto-resumes after the pause period ends.

### Cancel
- Cancel anytime from your account page.
- Your current box still ships if already processed.
- No cancellation fees, no hassle.

## Shipping Information
- **Free Shipping**: On all subscription orders.
- **Delivery Time**: 3-5 business days.
- **Packaging**: Ships in a discreet box. Cereal bags are resealable.
- **Delivery Areas**: US only (including Alaska and Hawaii).

## Billing
- **Payment**: Credit/debit card. HSA/FSA not applicable.
- **Billing Cycle**: On subscription date each month.
- **Receipts**: Available in account under Order History.
- **Failed Payments**: 3 retries over 5 days, then subscription paused.

## Satisfaction Guarantee
- If you don't love Magic Spoon, contact us within 30 days of your first order.
- We'll provide a full refund or free replacement.
- Guarantee applies to first order only.
