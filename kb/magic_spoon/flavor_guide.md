# Magic Spoon - Flavor Guide

## Current Flavors

### Fruity
A bold, sweet cereal with fruity notes. Zero sugar, 12g protein per serving.
- **Taste profile**: Sweet, fruity, similar to Froot Loops but less artificial.
- **Best for**: Fruity cereal lovers, kids, anyone wanting a nostalgic breakfast.
- **Best served**: With milk or as a dry snack.

### Frosted
A classic frosted cereal flavor. 0g sugar, 13g protein.
- **Taste profile**: Sweet vanilla-frosted flavor. Very close to Frosted Flakes.
- **Best for**: Those who love sweet, simple cereals.
- **Tip**: Let it sit in milk for 1 minute to soften slightly.

### Cocoa
Rich chocolate flavor. 0g sugar, 12g protein.
- **Taste profile**: Deep chocolate, similar to Cocoa Puffs.
- **Best for**: Chocolate lovers, dessert-for-breakfast crowd.
- **Pairing**: Top with sliced banana or strawberries.

### Cinnamon Roll
Warm cinnamon flavor. 0g sugar, 12g protein.
- **Taste profile**: Sweet cinnamon, like Cinnamon Toast Crunch.
- **Best for**: Fall mornings, cinnamon enthusiasts.
- **Pairing**: Add a splash of vanilla almond milk.

### Peanut Butter
Savory-sweet peanut butter taste. 0g sugar, 13g protein.
- **Taste profile**: True peanut butter flavor, lightly sweet.
- **Best for**: Peanut butter fans, high-protein breakfast.
- **Pairing**: Great with chocolate milk for a PB&J vibe.

### Blueberry Muffin
Light blueberry flavor with muffin notes. 0g sugar, 12g protein.
- **Taste profile**: Sweet blueberry with a hint of baked muffin.
- **Best for**: Fruit-forward cereal fans.
