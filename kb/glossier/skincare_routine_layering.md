# Glossier - Skincare Routine & Product Layering

## The Glossier Philosophy
Skincare should be fun, easy, and effective. Less is more—but the right products make all the difference.

## The Core Routine

### Step 1: Cleanse (AM & PM)
- **Milky Jelly Cleanser**: All-skin-type, non-stripping. Perfect first step.
- **Solution**: Exfoliating toner (PM only, 2-3x/week). Contains 2% salicylic acid + AHA/PHA.

### Step 2: Serum (AM & PM)
- **Super Bounce**: Hyaluronic acid + vitamin B5 for plump, hydrated skin.
- **Super Pure**: Niacinamide + zinc for balancing and soothing.
- **Super Glow**: Vitamin C + magnesium for brightening.

### Step 3: Moisturize (AM & PM)
- **Priming Moisturizer**: Lightweight, works under makeup. Rich in squalane.
- **Priming Moisturizer Rich**: Thicker formula for dry skin. Contains shea butter.
- **After Baume**: Barrier-supporting balm for compromised or very dry skin.

### Step 4: Protect (AM only)
- **Invisible Shield**: SPF 30-50. Weightless, no white cast. Chemical sunscreen.

## Product Layering Rules
1. **Thinnest to thickest**: Cleanser → Toner → Serum → Moisturizer → SPF
2. **Wait 60 seconds** between active ingredients (e.g., niacinamide → vitamin C)
3. **Do NOT mix**: Vitamin C with direct acids (salicylic/lactic) - use at different times
4. **Do NOT layer**: Retinol with physical exfoliants - irritation risk

## Ingredient Spotlight
- **Squalane**: Plant-derived (olive/sugarcane), mimics skin's natural sebum, non-comedogenic
- **Niacinamide**: Vitamin B3, regulates oil production, strengthens barrier
- **Vitamin C**: L-ascorbic acid derivative, stable formula, brightening + antioxidant
- **Salicylic Acid**: Beta-hydroxy acid, oil-soluble, penetrates pores for deeper exfoliation
- **Hyaluronic Acid**: Holds 1000x its weight in water, plumping hydration

## Sensitive Skin Modifications
- Patch test new products for 7 days
- Introduce one new product at a time
- Reduce Solution usage to 1x/week if irritation occurs
- Use After Baume as your final layer to seal moisture barrier
