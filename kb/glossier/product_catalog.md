# Glossier Product Catalog & Support Guide

## Milky Jelly Cleanser
- **Description:** A gentle yet effective creamy gel face wash.
- **Common Issues:** Pump failure, leaking cap, cloudy consistency (if exposed to extreme heat).
- **Troubleshooting:**
    - If pump is stuck, rotate 90 degrees counter-clockwise to unlock.
    - If leaking, ensure the inner seal was removed during first use.

## Balm Dotcom
- **Description:** A universal skin salve.
- **Common Issues:** Separation of oil and wax.
- **Troubleshooting:** Massage the tube before opening to re-emulsify the formula.

## Failure Analysis Dashboard Integration
All product-specific quality issues reported through the gTeam are automatically tagged and aggregated in the Hop Support Failure Analysis Dashboard. This allows our product team to identify batch-level defects (e.g., Milky Jelly Pump Batch #2026-06) within hours of the first reports.
