# Therapy Services at Talkspace
Talkspace provides a variety of online therapy options tailored to different life stages and needs.

## Types of Therapy
*   **Individual Therapy**: One-on-one sessions with a licensed therapist to address personal challenges such as anxiety, depression, stress, or life transitions.
*   **Couples Therapy**: For partners looking to improve communication, resolve conflicts, or strengthen their relationship.
*   **Teen Therapy (Ages 13-17)**: Specialized support for adolescents navigating the unique pressures of the teenage years. Parents or guardians must provide consent.
*   **Psychiatry & Medication Management**: Specialized medical care for mental health conditions. Psychiatric providers can perform evaluations, prescribe medications, and provide ongoing monitoring. (Note: Therapists do not prescribe medication).

## How it Works
1.  **Online Assessment**: Complete a brief questionnaire about your symptoms, preferences, and goals.
2.  **Matching**: Be matched with a licensed therapist in your state, usually within 48 hours.
3.  **Flexible Communication**: Connect with your therapist via:
    *   **Text Messaging**: Send unlimited messages to your therapist's secure room at any time.
    *   **Live Video Sessions**: Schedule real-time face-to-face appointments.
    *   **Audio/Video Messaging**: Leave voice or video notes for your provider.

## Effectiveness
Clinical research conducted in partnership with major institutions (e.g., Journal of Telemedicine and e-Health) has shown that Talkspace is as effective as traditional face-to-face therapy.
*   **70%** of members saw improvement in anxiety or depression symptoms within 3 months.
*   **80%** reported Talkspace was as or more effective than face-to-face therapy.

## Switching Therapists
If you don't feel a strong connection with your matched therapist, you can switch to a new provider at any time directly through your dashboard. We prioritize finding the right fit for your therapeutic journey.
