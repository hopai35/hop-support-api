# Insurance and Billing
Talkspace is committed to making mental health care affordable and accessible by prioritizing an insurance-first model.

## Insurance Coverage
Talkspace is in-network with many major health insurance plans, including:
*   Cigna
*   Anthem
*   UnitedHealthcare
*   Aetna
*   Blue Cross Blue Shield
*   Medicare (in select states)

**Most insured members pay a $0 copay**, or a standard copay typically around $15.

## How to Check Coverage
1.  During sign-up, enter your insurance information.
2.  Talkspace will automatically verify your benefits in real-time.
3.  You will see your estimated out-of-pocket cost (copay) before completing enrollment.

## Out-of-Pocket Pricing
If you do not have insurance or your plan is not currently in-network, Talkspace offers flexible subscription plans:
*   **Individual Therapy**: Starts at $69/week (billed monthly).
*   **Couples Therapy**: Pricing varies; check the dashboard for current plans.
*   **Psychiatry**: Billed per session (Initial evaluation + follow-up sessions).

## Employee Assistance Programs (EAP) and Employer Benefits
Many employers offer Talkspace as a free benefit.
*   **Employer Code**: If your company provides Talkspace directly, you can enter your unique employer code during sign-up.
*   **EAP**: Check with your HR department to see if Talkspace sessions are covered through your Employee Assistance Plan.

## Billing Cycles
Subscriptions are typically billed monthly. You can cancel or change your plan at any time through your account settings. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.
