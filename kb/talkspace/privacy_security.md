# Privacy, Security, and Trust at Talkspace
Talkspace is a healthcare company, and we treat your privacy and security as our top priority.

## HIPAA Compliance
Talkspace is fully HIPAA-compliant. We adhere to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act standards to protect your Protected Health Information (PHI).
*   **Encryption**: All communication between you and your therapist is encrypted. Messaging is protected using banking-grade 256-bit AES encryption.
*   **Secure Platform**: Our app and website utilize secure servers and industry-standard security protocols.
*   **Confidentiality**: Your conversations with your therapist are confidential. Information is only shared in extreme cases of safety concerns (e.g., intent to harm self or others) as required by law.

## Data Protection
*   **Authentication**: We recommend using a passcode or biometric lock (FaceID/Fingerprint) on the Talkspace mobile app for an extra layer of privacy.
*   **Anonymity**: While you must provide your identity for clinical and insurance purposes, you can choose a "Nickname" for your therapy room if you prefer.
*   **BBA Agreements**: All our partners and vendors are required to sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) to ensure they uphold our high security standards.

## Guardian Layer Integration (Hop Support)
Our AI-powered support agent (Hop Support) is designed with a specialized "Guardian Layer" for healthcare leads like Talkspace:
*   **Automated PHI Redaction**: The system automatically detects and redacts sensitive identifiers (names, IDs, specific medical terms) before data is processed by the AI model.
*   **Skyflow Vaulting**: Sensitive data is stored in a secure Skyflow vault. This allows for "reversible de-identification" — if a human clinical supervisor needs to review a case, the data can be securely re-identified, but it remains invisible to the underlying AI.
*   **Auditability**: Every AI interaction is logged and auditable to ensure compliance with medical data regulations.

## Patient Rights
You have the right to request a copy of your records or an accounting of disclosures at any time. For privacy-related questions, contact privacy@talkspace.com.
